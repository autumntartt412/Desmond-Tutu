Desmond Tutu

Desmond Tutu was a South African Anglican Bishop who was known for fighting against racism. His couragous and hard work was recognized around the world
for being a human rights activist. Here are some of the awards won by this honorable, South African preist during his 90 years of life.

LIFETIME ACHIEVMENT AWARDS
* Nobel Peace Prize
* Gandhi Peace Prize
* Alber Schweiter Prize for Humanitarianism
* Presidential Metal of Freedom
* Sydney Peace Prize
* Fulbright Prize

Desmond Tutu will always be rembembered for his gracious and humble acts of kindness and wisdom.
